Intentionally-small. Big ideas. Big experience.

Burris/Wagnon Architects, P.A. is an intentionally-small, award-winning design firm in Jackson, Mississippi, offering architecture, planning, and interior design services. Though small in number, we are large in agility, experience, technical expertise, ideas, and vision. From large-scale university campus projects to intimate residential projects, we design for functionality, durability, and sustainability. We design with purpose. We design with care. And we design to inspire.
