Kethley Hall Renovation

Cleveland, Mississippi

The Kethley Hall Renovation at Delta State University involved the complete re-design of the interior space to accommodate the College of Arts and Sciences. Exterior work included repair and cleaning of stone work and brick. A new roof was also installed. The new configuration includes state-of-the-art “smart” classrooms and lecture hall, department suites, and faculty offices. Budget constraints, an intimate understanding of the existing building, and thorough knowledge of the new program for the building helped determine a renovation strategy based on a thoughtful interweaving of existing and new construction.

The Renovation of Bailey Hall and the Union Building includes bringing both facilities into compliance with current standards and codes, as well as updating the mechanical system and providing a new roof system at the Union Building.

Completed 2007
Winner 2018 AIA Mississippi Merit Award
